Why You Should Use Us

Tired of going to laundromats and seeing all the machines occupied? We use GPS to locate the closest laundromat to you. By using our service we caln help you with the inconvience of waiting in a packed laundromat. Our service tells you if machines are available or in use, on top of that you can make reservations. This removes the human factor of interacting with people asking if they are done using a machine. Our simple web application meets the needs of any community by providing an easily accepsible system to locate, check availability, reserve, and pay for a machine.

Where can I use this

This application can be used in any community that have listed laundromat locations. Whether you live in a small town or a big city our services can help provide you with information to make your laundry quicker and simple.
